Mission Statetement
We are a group of computer scientists who want to make the world a bit better.
Although this particular piece of software is designed for blind and visually impaired children, it is open for everyone to use.
Our software is both free and educational, but using it constitutes your agreement to assume all risk associated with its use.


Games
Please your your arrow keys to move the square around the screen.
You can also press any other key on your keyboard to play a sound!
The most important rule is: have fun!
